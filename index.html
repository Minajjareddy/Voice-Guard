FAQ

Common
questions

Can't find your answer? Reach out to our team and we'll get back within 4 hours.


How accurate is the detection? +
VoiceGuard achieves 98.4% accuracy on our benchmark dataset comprising 50,000+ real and synthetic voice samples across 12 different TTS and voice conversion systems. Accuracy varies slightly by model — our Bi-LSTM+CNN ensemble performs best on unseen forgery tools.
What languages are supported? +
Full support for English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, and 14 other languages. Detection is largely language-agnostic since it operates on spectral and temporal features rather than linguistic content. Additional languages can be added on Enterprise plans.
Can it detect brand-new AI voice models? +
Our models are retrained monthly on newly released TTS systems. The spectral artifact detection generalizes well to unseen generators — in testing, we maintained 91%+ accuracy on voices generated by models released after our training cutoff.
Is my audio data stored? +
No audio is stored by default. All processing happens ephemerally and data is discarded after scoring. Enterprise customers can opt into secure storage for audit trails under their own encryption keys.
How do I integrate the API? +
A single REST endpoint accepts audio files or base64-encoded streams. SDKs are available for Python, Node.js, Java, and Go. Most integrations are complete within hours — see our documentation for sample code.
